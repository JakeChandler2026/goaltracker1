Goal Tracking Portal

Track progress, split work into sub-goals, and sign off completions.

Youth can log in, update sub-goal progress, repeat a checklist item for as many days as needed, and watch each goal move toward completion. Youth leaders work within Young Men or Young Women, while bishops can oversee both and manage goals across the whole ward.

Included in this build

  • Youth login
  • Youth leader login
  • Bishop login
  • Young Men and Young Women access rules
  • Goal and sub-goal tracking
  • Repeated checklist counts
  • Automatic percentage bars
  • Goal templates, copying, and editing
  • Youth leader approval workflow
